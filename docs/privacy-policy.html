Privacy Policy for Execute Python Extension

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1. Introduction

Execute Python ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Execute Python Chrome extension. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our extension and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: Execute Python does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers.

2.1 Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Extension settings and preferences (enabled/disabled state, button visibility options)
  • JupyterLite URL configuration
  • Site whitelist/blacklist preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. How We Use Information

The locally stored settings are used solely to:

  • Customize the extension behavior according to your preferences
  • Remember your configuration settings between browser sessions
  • Control which websites the extension operates on

4. Code Execution

Local Processing Only: All Python code execution happens entirely within your browser using Pyodide (WebAssembly). No code is sent to external servers for processing.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension may open JupyterLite (a client-side Jupyter environment) in new tabs. JupyterLite runs entirely in your browser and does not send data to external servers.

6. Data Security

Since no data is transmitted externally, your information remains secure on your local device. The extension operates entirely offline after the initial installation.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: phlpy.dev@gmail.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/philipontheroof/execute-python

9. No Cookies

This extension does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
